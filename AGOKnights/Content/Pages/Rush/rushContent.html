Rush 2016!

Welcome to Alpha's Rush page! Here is all the information you need to know about Alpha's Rush. Feel free to look at the other pages on this website, such as our About page and our Pictures page to learn more about Alpha.

Get to know our rush directors, Tanner Robbins and Sheldon Yeakley. They will get to know you and tell you everything you need to know about Alpha. You can contact them at ambassador@agoknights.com

Here's a list of our rush events:

  • September 6th - Club Night - 7pm, Hardeman Auditorium
    Hear speeches from all the club presidents, including Alpha president Hunter Edwards, meet the members of Alpha and get info about Alpha's upcoming rush events.

  • September 9th - Human Foosball/Armor Building - 5:30pm, Lawson Commons
    Come be a part of our annual game of life-size foosball! If you don't want to play, and just want to hang out we'll also be making armor for Joust.

  • September 11th - Alpha Softball Game #1 - 9:30pm, Intramural Fields
    Come and play Softball for Alpha or just cheer our team on!

  • September 13th - Alpha Softball Game #2 - 7:00pm, Intramural Fields
    Come and play Softball for Alpha or just cheer our team on!

  • September 14th - Roasting Marshmallows with Pi - 8:30pm, Forum
    Come and get cozy with Alpha and the ladies of Pi roasting marshmallows!

  • September 18th - Game Night - 7:00pm, Student Center
    In classic Alpha fashion, come enjoy a night of board/card games in the Student Center.

  • September 18th - Alpha Softball Game #3 - 9:30pm, Intramural Fields
    Come and play Softball for Alpha or just cheer our team on!

  • September 20th - Alpha Open Meeting - 6:30pm, DAH 217
    Come and see Alpha members in their natural habitat during our open meeting.

  • September 22nd - Scavenger Hunt - 6:30pm, Pavillion
    Who doesn't love searching for random objects? Come and scavenge items from around campus with us!

  • September 24th - Joust - 2:00pm, Lawson Commons
    Put your cardboard armor-making skills to the test in this epic tournament for honor. There will be blood! (And by blood, we mean shaving cream)

  • September 27th - Interviews - 6:00, DAH 217
    Come interview with the officers of Alpha to be inducted as a Knight of Alpha Gamma Omega. We'd love to have you!

  • September 30th - Induction Night - 5:30pm, Gaylord Forum
    Now that you're officially a full Alpha member, enjoy a grand knight's feast, receive your knight name, and celebrate your induction into Alpha Gamma Omega!
